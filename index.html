How to play

This is a Hot or Cold Number Guessing Game. The game goes like this:

  • 1. Click New Game.
  • 2. I pick a random secret number between 1 to 100 and keep it hidden.
  • 3. You need to guess until you can find the hidden secret number.
  • 4. You will get feedback on how close ("hot") or far ("cold") your guess is.
